Frequently Asked Questions

Guarantee of satisfaction

Our company is dedicated to providing the best experience at the lowest cost. Our reservation is guaranteed after it has been placed. If there are any changes or cancellations that need to be made, please make one week in advance to allow schedules to be rearranged. Any sooner and you may be charged a small fee.

How old do my children have to be to participate?

Our activities are not for all ages. Young children may have a difficult time and could be at risk of injury. We are not responsible for any injuries come to any of our customers. We ask that children under the age of eight cannot participate in any of our activities. Anyone who has medical problems will need to fill out one of our forms to allow us to accommodate to your needs and pace.

Where can I send my questions to get a direct answer?

Any questions that you may have can be sent via email. We will respond within one business day. You can receive quicker answers by calling our toll-free number below. By booking a reservation, you will need to pay in advance to ensure that our guides will be paid. A receipt will be emailed to you with a verification number that will be needed when you arrive at the lodge. If you enjoyed your experience, we invite you to leave a Google review telling us about your experience.

Our Commitment

We are committed to improving the quality of our service and providing you the best experience possible. Thank you for allowing us to show you the expanded outdoors!
